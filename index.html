How ApnaKitchen Works

1

Browse & Select

Explore a variety of delicious homemade dishes from verified local cooks in Swabi.

2

Customize Your Order

Select quantity, add extras like ketchup, raita, or cheese, and provide special instructions.

3

Secure Payment

Pay securely through our platform. A 5% platform fee is collected from both cook and rider per order.

4

Cook Prepares

The cook receives your order instantly, prepares your food fresh, and marks it ready for pickup.

5

Rider Delivery

Once payment is confirmed to the platform, a nearby rider picks up your order and delivers it.

6

Review & Enjoy

Rate your experience and leave reviews for the cook. Your feedback helps maintain quality.
